Meeting Minutes

Unfinished Business: The last meeting's minutes were approved with notes on progress made in development and marketing strategies since.

New Business: Discussed potential features for the GTA VI launch, including enhanced AI behavior, expanded multiplayer modes, and deeper world interaction. A list of launch cities was also debated.

The team reviewed various marketing proposals for the release period, including interactive social media campaigns, partnerships with influencers, and exclusive content for pre-orders.

Development milestones were evaluated, whilst finalizing mechanics and the integration of user feedback from beta testers.

Release Date Planning: Several potential release dates were considered, aligning with major holidays. A follow-up meeting on 05/11 was scheduled to finalize the release date after consulting with production team.

Other Business: The floor was opened for any other concerns. A brief discussion took place regarding post-launch support and updates.

It was agreed that all departments should prioritize transparency with the gaming community to build trust.
